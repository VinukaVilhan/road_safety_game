Two Learning Modes

Theory Test MCQ

  • 6 knowledge categories
  • 70% pass threshold
  • Attempts, best score, unlock chains
  • Road Signs: intro → learn → MCQ → minigame
  • Image-based sign questions

Driving Test Simulation

  • Flame 2D engine + Tiled maps
  • Steering, pedals, gearbox (P, 1–4, R)
  • Turn signals (double/triple tap)
  • Zone-based rules & scoring (0–100)
  • Driving reports with violation screenshots

Key Features

  • Firebase auth (email, Google, Facebook)
  • Cloud progress sync (Firestore + local Isar DB)
  • AI Virtual Instructor (Gemini) on every screen
  • Photo upload for sign identification
  • Odometer — total miles driven on profile
  • In-game radio tuner & phone music playback
  • Level briefings & story dialogs
  • Controls tutorial (4 lessons, no progress tracking)
  • Sound, vibration, and rule-break feedback
  • Swiss-style UI across all platforms
  • Android, iOS, Web, Windows, macOS, Linux

Progression & Feedback

Unlock system

  • Linear chains — complete A to unlock B
  • Theory: pass at 70%+
  • Driving: rule checklist pass/fail
  • Lock icons & difficulty colors on level cards

After each drive

  • Score 0–100 with breakdown
  • Checklist of rules followed / broken
  • Optional screenshot upload (Cloudinary)
  • Retry or return to level list

Not included (future scope)

Shop · achievements · leaderboards · multiplayer · power-ups · car customization
